SEC Survivor - Basketball

Conference games only. Each week, pick one SEC team and ride with that team for both games.

  • 2-0: safe
  • 1-1: safe, but one strike
  • 0-2: eliminated immediately
  • Three strikes also eliminates you
  • Teams may not be reused
  • Teams with only one game in a normal week are not eligible
  • Week 1 is opening Saturday only; everyone receives a ghost midweek win, so nobody can be eliminated
